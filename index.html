About Me

Good design is (among other things): intuitive. refreshing. comforting. invisible. I want to bring you good design. But first, let's get to know each other.

I fall asleep best after a bowl of mint chocolate chip ice cream.

I studied communication and studio art in school, dabbling in some code along the way. Working as a TA for digital design and photography courses, I built up skillsets in Adobe software and honed my creative eye.

I create print designs as well as digital and web designs. And, of course, write 'em up and ship 'em out in good old reliable code.
